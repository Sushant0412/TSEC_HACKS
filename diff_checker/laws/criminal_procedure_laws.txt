1. Every accused person is presumed innocent until proven guilty.
2. The right to legal representation is guaranteed under Article 22 of the Indian Constitution.
3. Any confession obtained under coercion is inadmissible in court.
4. The right to remain silent is protected under Article 20(3) of the Indian Constitution.
5. Police custody cannot exceed 24 hours without judicial approval (Section 167 CrPC).
6. Double jeopardy is prohibited under Article 20(2) of the Indian Constitution.
