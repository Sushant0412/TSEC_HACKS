1. A contract must have a lawful object as per Section 23 of the Indian Contract Act, 1872.
2. All agreements without consideration are void, as per Section 25 of the Indian Contract Act, 1872.
3. A contract must have free consent, as per Section 14 of the Indian Contract Act, 1872.
4. An agreement in restraint of trade is void, as per Section 27 of the Indian Contract Act, 1872.
5. A minor cannot enter into a contract, as per Section 11 of the Indian Contract Act, 1872.
6. Agreements based on fraud, misrepresentation, coercion, or undue influence are voidable, as per Sections 17, 18, 15, and 16 respectively.
